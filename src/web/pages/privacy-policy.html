Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

ExpenseSyncBot ("the Bot", "we", "us") is a Telegram bot that helps users track expenses and sync them to Google Sheets. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect and store the following data when you use the Bot:

  • Telegram identifiers — your Telegram user ID, first name, and the group chat ID where the Bot is used. We do not collect your phone number or Telegram username.
  • Expense data — amounts, currencies, categories, and comments you send to the Bot.
  • Google OAuth refresh token — when you connect a Google account, we store an encrypted refresh token to maintain access to Google Sheets on your behalf.
  • AI conversation history — messages sent to the Bot's AI assistant within group chats, used to provide contextual responses.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Creating and updating Google Sheets — we use Google Sheets API (spreadsheets scope) to create a spreadsheet and write your expenses into it.
  • Creating spreadsheet files — we use Google Drive API (drive.file scope) solely to create new spreadsheet files. We do not access any other files on your Google Drive.
  • Expense tracking — we store expenses locally to provide statistics, budgets, and AI-powered analysis.

3. Google API Scopes

The Bot requests the following Google API scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets — to read and write expense data in your Google Sheets spreadsheet.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file — to create new spreadsheet files. This scope is limited to files created by the Bot and does not grant access to any other files on your Drive.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or Google user data with any third parties. We do not use your data for advertising, retargeting, or interest-based advertising. We do not use your data to determine credit-worthiness or for lending purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in a SQLite database on a private server with restricted access.
  • Google refresh tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage. Plaintext tokens are never logged or stored.
  • All communication with Google APIs and the OAuth callback endpoint is encrypted via HTTPS (TLS).
  • Server access is restricted to authorized administrators only.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as you actively use the Bot. Expense records are kept to provide statistics and analytics.

  • When you revoke Google access (via /reconnect or Google Account permissions), we delete your stored Google refresh token.
  • You can request complete deletion of all your data — including expenses, tokens, and conversation history — at any time by contacting us (see below).
  • If you stop using the Bot and do not interact with it for an extended period, your data remains stored unless you explicitly request deletion.

7. Your Rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Disconnect Google account — use the /reconnect command in Telegram or revoke access directly in your Google Account permissions.
  • Request data deletion — contact us to request complete deletion of all your data.
  • Access your data — all your expense data is available in the Google Sheets spreadsheet created by the Bot and via the /stats command in Telegram.

8. Google API Services User Data Policy

ExpenseSyncBot's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes to how we use Google user data, we will notify users and obtain fresh consent before using data in the new way. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.

11. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to request data deletion, contact us via Telegram: @ExpenseSyncBot.
