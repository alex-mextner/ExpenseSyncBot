ExpenseSyncBot

A Telegram bot for tracking expenses and syncing them to Google Sheets.

What It Does

ExpenseSyncBot lets you record expenses directly in a Telegram group chat. It automatically syncs every expense to a Google Sheets spreadsheet, giving you a real-time financial overview.

Features

  • Multi-currency expense tracking with automatic conversion
  • Automatic sync to Google Sheets
  • Smart category detection
  • Budget management and alerts
  • AI-powered expense analysis and advice
  • Receipt photo scanning (OCR)

How It Uses Google APIs

When you connect your Google account, the Bot creates a dedicated Google Sheets spreadsheet and writes your expenses into it. It uses two Google API scopes:

  • Google Sheets API — to read and write expense data in the spreadsheet
  • Google Drive API (drive.file) — to create new spreadsheet files. This is limited to files the Bot creates and does not access any other files on your Drive.

You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account permissions.
